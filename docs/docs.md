<h2>Overview</h2>
Our compiler serves as a more hands on approach to the Rutgers CS111 Pseudocode utilized within the first few weeks of the semester, and ultimately what will be tested on.
Having dealt with the struggle of truly being able to understand without a computer processing the code, relying on human processes leaves room
for only error and confusion, therefore we have created a solution that aims to help thousands and hopefully millions of students that venture into the vast sea of Computer Science.
